---
title: Don't use black in your designs
tags: [design, css]
---

Use these alternatives instead:

| Name          | Hex     | RGB             |
| ------------- | ------- | --------------- |
| Oil black     | #0c0c0c | rgb(12, 12, 12) |
| Obsidian      | #0b1215 | rgb(11, 18, 21) |
| Night         | #121212 | rgb(18, 18, 18) |
| Charcoal grey | #171717 | rgb(23, 23, 23) |
| Rick black    | #04041b | rgb(4, 4, 27)   |
| Mignight blue | #101720 | rgb(16, 23, 32) |
| Dark blue     | #05091e | rgb(5, 9, 30)   |
